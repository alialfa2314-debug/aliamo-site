About

The maker
behind the
design

I'm Ali Mohammadi — a Fashion Design student at STF Zürich with nearly two decades of hands-on experience in bespoke tailoring and bridal wear.

Currently interning at Pablo Reininger and Social Fabric, I bring a perspective most designers don't have: I've spent twenty years understanding how fabric moves, how a seam holds, and what makes a garment truly fit.

I love combining creativity and functionality in design — always looking for ways to deepen and expand my craft.
